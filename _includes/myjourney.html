My Journey

My Love and Passion is to offer the space to find your Inner Peace, through the Action of your Breath. We take up to 24,000 Breaths a day, so make everyone a good one. Remember, Paul Says “Just Breath” and find your inner power.

  • Gym and Food

    In my early 20’s I began to understand the importance of nutrition and fitness, by keeping yourself in shape physically. The flesh and bones that are part of us came from the earth we walk on, it needs to be fed from the food sources found in mother earth. To would only consume high energy natural foods and steer away from processed sources which gave great results. Exercising the body was also important, remaining flexible and supple. I spent like many do, hours down the gym watching my body change from exercise and the foods I consumed.

  • Karate

    Having always loved Martial Arts I found a random Wado Ryu Karate club near my home . The teacher was Japanese, Masafumi Shiomitsu Sensei 7th Dan, in degrees of lineage, it was him and then the founder of Wado Ryu. Lucky me. I trained with him for many years, the punching and kicking was great exercise. But there I was introduced to Meditation, Mokuso-Empty Mind. At the beginning of every class we would practice this and I continued as much as I could at home. From this my awareness grew and my fears disappeared, until it gave me the ability to feel what my opponent was about to do before he had done it. It was in our warm ups and stretching, I suddenly found that we can place tension in our bodies even when we were stretching. So at this point by returning to the breath a state of relaxation extends to the whole body. The breath is our Superpower.

  • Mediumship & Healing

    Many years passed and I met my beautiful wife Helen in my late 30's, we had so much in common having unusual experiences as children and adults. Feeling seeing and experiencing unexplainable things. So together we explored the world of mediumship leading us to meet teachers, Glyn Edwards, Eileen Davies, Jackie Brian and many more, we had some amazing experiences with the unseen world. It was also at this time I realised I had natural healing ability within me, always being interested in the bodies energy and how it affected us. Realising the bodies energy is more important than the flesh and bones part us. This healing would be hands on or a few words to a stranger, it had some dramatic affects. But in this exploration I found the breath could affect our consciousness and the flow of energy in our bodies, realising there is a lot lot more to this.

  • Ki Aikido

    Following on from this I suddenly felt the calling of Marshall Arts again, not being able to find an art that included true spirituality, I came across Ki Aikido (Shin Shin Ki Aikido). Here with the help of Packman Sensei and Sindon Sensei a true understanding of body movement with mind and body unification can be achieved and a non-conflict way of life is understood. Here I gained my 2nd degree black belt which enabled me to teach others in Ki Aikido. Once again the common factor of the Breath, it allows us to live a centred way of life keeping mind and body unified.

  • Kriya Yoga

    Around the same time I met Swami Aliananda Paramahansa teacher of Kriya Yoga, she had travelled to India (the Himalayas) to find Kriya Yoga from the source, Maha Avatar Babaji. The same Babaji as in Swami Paramahansa Yogananda’ s book, Auto Biography of a Yogi. After visiting Aliananda many times and being shown the Kriya Breaths and Meditation, she asked me to be a teacher for Kriya Yoga. Here I gained a true awareness of the self. This self is in everyone, the realisation of it allows one to come from their truth, from their spirit, where your powers lie. Suddenly being an observer of this world and not participating in the plays and dramas that everyone becomes attached to. Once again with the aid of the breath, solutions appeared before the problems appeared.

The most amazing thing I suddenly realized, all my Teachers had offered me the space to find My Breath and this allowed me to step into my Truth my Peace.
We can only push the physical body so far. With some simple breathing technics, allowing the mind to still and with meditation, we can release those unwanted tensions. Allowing the bodies energy to flow as it should, bringing an ease to life keeping disease at bay. Nowhere for anxiety, headaches, joint pain and suffering. When we feed are body with foods of the earth, use the power of the mind and breath for us, not against us. True Miracles happen, you become that change you want to see in the world. Supreme unification, True Yoga. Keep it simple, Breathe, let go and surrender to your inner power. This is the story so far, tomorrow is a new day. Remember. Paul Says "Just Breathe”
